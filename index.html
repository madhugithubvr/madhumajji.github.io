About Me

Hi there! My name is Madhu and I am a postdoctoral researcher at MIT. I have a passion for discovering new things and constantly learning about the world around me. In my research, I focus on developing materials for applicatioons in sustainable energy and health using colloidal science principles. I have always been curious and driven to learn as much as I can, and my work as a researcher allows me to indulge this curiosity on a daily basis. I am constantly amazed by the depth and complexity of the world we live in, and I feel fortunate to have the opportunity to contribute to our understanding of it. In my free time, I enjoy watching documentaries, taking strolls in nature, and finding new ways to expand my knowledge and understanding of the world. I believe that learning is a lifelong pursuit, and I am always looking for new opportunities to grow and discover.
